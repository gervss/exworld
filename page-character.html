Human

  •    

The human race are a complex race of warriors, descendants of the soldiers of the old world. Having battle hardened techniques and mastery of the dark arts has allowed them to become a major power house in the continent of Tumac. They are skilled in many aspects of combat and life skilling. If players were looking for a more balanced gameplay then choosing the human race would be the ideal choice.
